ABOUT US

MIZO is our name. We are a cafe and laundrette located in the heart of Bristol's cultural district. We're passionate about bringing people together around the shared need to wash laundry. People gather here to meet, wash their clothes, receive much-needed nutrition, share stories, and develop true community, much as they would around any communities but with a twist.
